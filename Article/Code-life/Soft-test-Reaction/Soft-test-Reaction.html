Soft test reaction

发布于：2018/11/8 20:05 | 修改次数：0|标签：代码
I think the first step of the Soft test is to provide the reason and background for the project. The second step of test arrangement and plan: mainly to provide guidance for the whole test, the third step of functional testing to prove that the function of the program is normal, the fourth part of performance testing is mainly to provide quality assurance for the program. To sum up, the first step is to explain the reasons for the implementation of the project background project, the second step is to formulate the project plan, the third step is to confirm that the project can be used, and the fourth step is to ensure that the project quality is too hard.

软件测试感

发布于：2018/11/8 20:05 | 修改次数：0|标签：代码
我觉得软件测试第一步项目简介：主要是给项目提供原因和背景。第二步测试安排和计划：主要是为了给整个测试提供 指导，第三步功能测试证明程序的功能使用正常，第四部性能测试主要是给这个程序提供质量保证。综合起来就是第一 步讲明执行项目的原因背景项目，第二步制定项目计划，第三步确认项目可以使用，第四步保证项目质量过硬。

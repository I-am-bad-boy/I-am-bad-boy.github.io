Why I write blog

发布于：2018/11/3 17:05 | 修改次数：0|标签：感悟
I used to write a blog on csdn, but I gave up before long. At that time, I thought it was because I was too tired to write a blog, but now I know it was because I thought my time was limitless and I didn't think it was necessary to write a blog.

The time passed by me, except for the gradual beard and no trace left. Looking ahead, there is no retreat in front of you. Have I been white for the past 19 years? There is no trace of me in the world, as if I have been from the future, I am not resigned to it. Fortunately, my life has only passed a quarter, so I rewrote my blog, not because I wanted to leave a trace (which is not a trace), but because I wanted to supervise myself not to be muddled.

I write at least one blog every day to record my time.Come on!

我写博客的原因

发布于：2018/11/3 17:05 | 修改次数：0|标签：感悟
以前在csdn上写博客，没写多久就放弃了，那时我以为是因为写博客太累，但现在我知道是因为那时以为自己时光无限 觉得没有写博客的必要。
时光决绝的从我身边流过，除了渐渐扎人的胡须，没有留下任何痕迹。往前看前方已无退路，再回首身后已是茫茫。难 道我以前19年的都白过呢吗？这个世界上没有我的痕迹，仿佛我从未来过一样，我不甘心。还好我的人生才过了1/4， （去他的，已经过了1/4了）所以我又重新写博客了，不是因为想要留下痕迹（这也算不上痕迹），只是想监督自己不 要浑浑噩噩。
从今天起每天至少写一篇博客记录我的时间,加油!

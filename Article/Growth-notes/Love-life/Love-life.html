Love life

发布于：2018/11/7 21:27 | 修改次数：0|标签：成长笔记
I do not think whether I can succeed or not.
Since I have chosen the distance,
I will only take care of the wind and rain.
I do not think about whether I can win love.
Since I love roses,
I will bravely express my sincerity.
I don't think about whether there will be cold wind and rain behind me.
Since the goal is the horizon,
the only thing left to the world is the back.
I don't think the future is flat or muddy.
As long as we love life, everything is expected.

热爱生命

发布于：2018/11/3 17:05 | 修改次数：0|标签：成长笔记 汪国真
我不去想是否能够成功，
既然选择了远方便只顾风雨兼程。
我不去想是否能赢得爱情，
既然钟情于玫瑰，
就勇敢的的吐露真诚。
我不去想是否身后会袭来寒风冷雨，
既然目标是地平线，
留给世界的只能是背影。
我不会想未来是平坦还是泥泞，
只要热爱生命，
一切都在预料之中。
